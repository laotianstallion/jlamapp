ABOUT JULIE

Julie Lam is a professional makeup artist covering the St. Paul / Minneapolis area. A native Minnesotan (but without the accent), Julie’s passion and intuitive proficiency for her craft is evident in her ability to draw on her interdisciplinary experience to create the *right* look.

With experience in all aspects of media makeup, Julie easily adapts to the creative environment of every project she takes on. Her relaxed persona belies the perfectionist beneath—a controlled approach which results in a subject at ease and a look that fits the project specifications.

Julie’s educational background includes not only cosmetology licensure, but also graphic and web design. The concepts taught in these disciplines (color theory, Gestalt principles, classical art) are woven into Julie's practical experience along with tidbits gleaned from professional growth opportunities with field experts.

Her infectious enthusiasm for continuous development, paired with her talent for recognizing and enhancing natural beauty, ensures that clients receive consistent, professional service that meets their objectives.

In addition to being the epitome of sugar, spice and everything nice, Julie is a rockstar video gamer. When she’s not beating the boys in Call of Duty, Julie is available to travel on location.
